About Us

For over fifty years, Millbrook Furniture Solutions has been crafting unique custom‑built furniture and joinery from our base in Hastings, Hawke’s Bay. We collaborate with some of New Zealand’s leading architects, specifiers and businesses to produce bespoke pieces for office and home environments. Our small team of cabinetmakers draws on more than 90 years of combined trade experience, using proven traditional techniques and the finest materials to deliver beautiful, functional pieces.

We believe that exceptional craftsmanship should be matched by an equally exceptional customer experience. From concept to completion, our service is personal and professional, ensuring every project runs smoothly.

Our Services

Commercial Solutions

Working alongside leading architects and designers, our commercial work spans one‑off pieces through to complete office fit‑outs, reception areas, board and meeting rooms, staff areas and kitchens. We relish complex challenges and are experienced in producing intricate, high‑specification furniture and fittings.

Unique & Commissioned

Our craftsmen excel at creating exceptional custom‑made furniture and one‑off pieces from solid timber, veneers and laminates. We love designing special items that will last for generations and take pride in the passion and artistry that shines through every project.

Residential Design

From kitchens and vanity units to audio‑visual and wall units, Millbrook can customise every aspect of your home’s joinery. Our residential projects range from simple to sublime and basic to bold. Have an idea? Let’s talk.
